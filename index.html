What is SHO

SHO stands for SchoolHeadOffice.co.za, a CRM app for schools, parents, and the community. It simplifies school management and enhances the educational experience for everyone involved.

Our History

SchoolHeadOffice.co.za was originally started in 2016 under the name SchoolSpun.com.
It was developed by our founder Kagiso Eugene Sebogodi. The goal was to create a platform that could streamline the administrative tasks of schools while also engaging parents and the community in the educational process. SchoolSPUN.com offered various features that allowed users to raise money through donations for their children in school. It also enabled schools to directly raise funds from community members online with just a few clicks.

In 2024, after recognizing the potential for a more comprehensive solution, SchoolSpun.com was rebranded as SchoolHeadOffice.co.za. The new name reflects our broader vision of providing a centralized platform for school management. We retained the popular features of SchoolSpun, such as the donation-raising capabilities, and integrated them into a more advanced CRM system. This evolution allowed us to create a solution that addresses the daily activities and needs of schools and parents in a single app.

Features

SchoolHeadOffice.co.za offers a range of features designed to simplify school management and enhance communication between schools, parents, and the community:

  • Easy fundraising through donations for students and schools
  • Direct online fund-raising from community members
  • Comprehensive CRM for managing student, family, and school administration
  • Tools to boost school performance and streamline administrative tasks
  • Capabilities to grow the school's social media presence
  • Options to generate additional revenue for schools
  • Enhanced communication and engagement with parents and the community

Our Mission

Our mission at SchoolHeadOffice.co.za is to simplify school management and empower schools to provide exceptional value to students and parents. By leveraging our advanced CRM platform, schools can focus on delivering quality education while we handle the administrative workload. Our solution aims to boost school performance, enhance community engagement, and support the financial sustainability of educational institutions.
